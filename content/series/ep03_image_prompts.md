## ChatGPT에 붙일 지시문

아래 6개 장면을 각각 따로 이미지로 만들어줘.

중요:
- 한 장짜리 6컷 콜라주 금지
- 각 장면은 독립된 이미지 1장씩
- 쇼츠용 세로 9:16
- 현실적이고 따뜻한 한국 가정 분위기
- 부모와 아이가 함께 AI를 배우는 장면
- 이미지 안에 글자, 숫자, 자막 넣지 말 것
- 컴퓨터 화면에도 읽을 수 있는 글자 넣지 말 것
- 실존 인물, 브랜드, 영화, 아이돌, 로고 금지
- 상자공작, 로봇 만들기, 종이공예 장면 금지
- 아이가 AI로 성공하는 장면이 아니라, AI에게 다시 말하며 배우는 장면
- 손가락은 자연스럽게, 손목 연결 정확하게, 손가락 5개, 왜곡 금지
- 과장된 만화풍보다 따뜻한 현실풍

### 1. 결과를 보고 갸웃하는 아이
A warm realistic vertical 9:16 scene of a Korean child sitting at a desk with a parent nearby, looking slightly puzzled but curious at an AI-generated picture on a computer screen. The child is thinking, not disappointed. Cozy home learning environment, soft evening light, parent gently watching beside the child. No readable text on the screen, no brand logos, no famous people. Natural hands, realistic five fingers, correct wrist connection, no distorted fingers.

### 2. 부족한 부분을 찾는 장면
A realistic vertical 9:16 scene of a Korean child gently pointing at one small part of an AI-generated image on a computer screen, noticing what could be improved. A parent sits beside the child with a calm encouraging expression. The mood is thoughtful and educational. No readable text, no captions, no logos, no famous people. Natural hands and fingers, realistic wrist connection, no extra fingers.

### 3. 다시 요청하는 아이
A realistic vertical 9:16 scene of a Korean child speaking or typing a new request to an AI on a computer, with a parent beside them encouraging the child. The scene should show the child learning how to ask again, not passively watching. Cozy desk, warm light, gentle parent-child learning mood. No readable text on screen, no brand logos, no famous people. Natural hands, five fingers, realistic wrist connection.

### 4. 결과가 조금 좋아진 장면
A realistic vertical 9:16 scene of a Korean child and parent comparing a newly improved AI-generated picture on a computer screen. The child looks pleased because the result became a little closer to what they wanted. The parent smiles gently, showing that small improvement matters. No readable text, no logos, no famous people. Warm home learning atmosphere, natural hands and fingers.

### 5. 다시 말해보는 과정의 노트
A realistic vertical 9:16 close-up scene of a child's notebook beside a computer, showing a simple learning process through icons only: think, ask, look, improve, ask again. Avoid readable Korean or English text. Use blank sticky notes, simple arrows, and small doodle-like icons. A child's hand with natural five fingers holds a pencil. Cozy educational desk setting, no logos, no brands.

### 6. 부모와 아이가 함께 결과를 보는 엔딩
A realistic vertical 9:16 ending scene of a Korean child and parent smiling together while looking at a finished AI-created image on a computer. The feeling is not "AI did it for the child," but "the child learned to explain their idea better." Cozy home, soft light, calm proud mood. No readable text, no brand logos, no famous people. Natural hands, realistic five fingers, correct wrist connection.

## 생성 후 6점 체크 (하나라도 틀리면 그 장면만 재생성)
1. 6컷 콜라주가 아니라 각각 1장인가?
2. 이미지 안 글자가 없는가?
3. 손이 이상하지 않은가?
4. 공작/로봇/상자 만들기로 빠지지 않았는가?
5. AI에게 다시 말하는 과정이 보이는가?
6. 실존 인물/브랜드/로고가 없는가?
